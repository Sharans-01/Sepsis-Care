Why Early Detection Matters ?

Sepsis is a leading cause of preventable death. Each hour without proper treatment increases the risk of fatality. Our AI-powered system helps identify early signs—saving time, and most importantly, saving lives.

  • 🕒 Every hour of delay increases mortality by 7%
  • 🔍 AI identifies subtle patterns that doctors may miss
  • 📈 Early treatment reduces ICU admissions by up to 40%
